CUSTOMS CLEARANCE

Our professional staff are skilled in customs law, regulations and procedures for all countries in East Africa. We provide consultancy services based on our experience as well continuous development and training for our staff to ensure up to date knowledge of customs regulations. Customs Broker providing efficient and trouble free customs clearance and documentation services for all types of products, whether by air freight or sea freight. We offer personalized services to our clients and always spend time with them to ensure that all their requirements are met and all administration is handled professionally and efficiently. Our Customs Brokerage Fees are competitive while covering all necessary legal and logistical requirements. We take care of customs clearance for all the import and export consignments. Our Customs Services Includes:

  • Customs Clearance and Documentation
  • Door-to-Door from anywhere in the world
  • Sea Freight Customs Clearance and Documentation the Port of Mombasa
  • Air Freight Customs Clearance and Documentation from JKIA
  • Direct Computer Link to Customs and Kenya Revenue Authority through the Simba System
  • Direct Computer Link to Kenya Ports Authority through KWATOS
